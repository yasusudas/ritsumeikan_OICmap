Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Ritsumeikan OIC Map handles personal information and data.

Last updated: May 11, 2026

1. Operator

Ritsumeikan OIC Map (the "Service") is an unofficial service developed and operated by an individual. It has no relationship with Ritsumeikan University or any related organization.

2. Information Collected

The Service may collect the following information for service improvement and stable operation.

  • Access analytics information such as page views, referrers, device type, browser, OS, and region
  • Information entered into the contact form
  • Language settings and editor draft data stored in the browser

3. Purpose of Use

Collected information is used for the following purposes.

  • To provide the Service display, search, and editing features
  • To understand usage, improve features, and investigate defects
  • To respond to inquiries and provide necessary communication
  • To prevent unauthorized use and excessive access

4. Analytics

The Service uses Vercel Web Analytics. Vercel Web Analytics collects aggregate information such as page views and usage environment. According to Vercel's official documentation, Web Analytics records anonymous data points that are not tied to individuals or IP addresses and does not use third-party cookies.

Vercel Web Analytics privacy information: https://vercel.com/docs/analytics/privacy-policy

5. Cookies and Local Storage

The Service uses browser local storage for language settings, editor draft storage, and related features. This data is stored in the user's browser and can be deleted by the user.

When opening external services such as Google Forms, those external services may use cookies or similar technologies. Please review each external service's policy for how it manages information.

6. Third-Party Disclosure

Except where required by law, collected information will not be provided to third parties without the user's consent. However, information may be sent to external services within the scope necessary to operate the Service, including analytics, hosting, and the contact form.

7. External Links

The Service may include links to university materials, Google Forms, and other external sites. The operator of the Service is not responsible for the handling of personal information on external sites.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be changed without prior notice. The revised policy becomes effective when posted on this page.

9. Contact

For inquiries about this Privacy Policy, please use "Contact" on the top page.
