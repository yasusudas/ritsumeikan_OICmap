Access to Osaka Ibaraki Campus

2-150 Iwakura-cho, Ibaraki, Osaka 567-8570, Japan

Inquiries about access to Osaka Ibaraki Campus

TEL +81-72-665-2020 (Campus Information)

Access

  • ■From JR Kyoto Station

    12 minutes by JR, transfer at Takatsuki for 5 minutes, get off at Ibaraki Station, then about 5 minutes on foot

  • ■From JR Osaka Station

    14 minutes by JR, get off at Ibaraki Station, then about 5 minutes on foot

  • ■From Hankyu Kyoto-kawaramachi Station

    25 minutes by Hankyu, transfer at Ibaraki-shi for 2 minutes, get off at Minami-Ibaraki Station, then about 10 minutes on foot

  • ■From Hankyu Osaka-Umeda Station

    18 minutes by Hankyu, get off at Minami-Ibaraki Station, then about 10 minutes on foot

  • ■From Hankyu Nishinomiya-kitaguchi Station

    9 minutes by Hankyu, transfer at Juso for 14 minutes, get off at Minami-Ibaraki Station, then about 10 minutes on foot

  • ■From Osaka Monorail Kadomashi Station

    13 minutes by monorail, get off at Minami-Ibaraki Station, then about 10 minutes on foot

  • ■From Osaka Monorail Osaka Airport Station

    21 minutes by monorail, get off at Unobe Station, then about 10 minutes on foot
